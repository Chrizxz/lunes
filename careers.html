Open Roles


In Lunes, we are always open to hiring people that seeks a bright future. We are open to hiring people at an entry-level whom are interested in the fields of Computer Engineering, Business, Marketing, Programming, and more. We are open either part-time, full-time, or even contract work.

If you are interested in strengthening your resume or gathering the experience of working at a cloud hosting company then feel free to reach out to my discord: Чеён#7428. 

Please be aware that (at the moment), these are currently “volunteer” positions. However, in the future it is planned for workers to be paid respectfully in some manner. In addition, we can help you expand your resume / work experience. 

IT Support


IT Support helps the community by answering to support tickets and offering advice on the discord bot running. This work requires patience and strong communication skills. There is an expectation of respect when it comes to this position.

Responsibilities 
> Responds to Support Tickets on the Discord platform

Qualifications / Requirements
> Must be able to work in a professional manner
> Must have some experience regarding coding
> That's it!

Bonus Requirements (Optional)
> Experience with NodeJs


IT Engineer


IT Engineer helps with the community regarding server problems specifically in helping users with getting their bots working and helping with getting the server functioning at an efficient manner. In addition, Technician Support would be asked to help with a few projects time-to-time from a Project Manager. 

Responsibilities
> Help with Individual’s Problems
> Operate and Work on Projects 

Qualifications / Requirements
> Must be able to work in a professional manner
> That’s it! 

Bonus Requirements (Optional)
> Experience with NodeJs

Project Manager



Project Manager focuses on either getting projects tasks or requesting them. From there, they work with IT Engineers on finishing those projects on a schedule. This role requires great leadership skills.

Responsibilities
> Get Projects Done on a Timely Manner
> Communicate with others 
> Update on Progress to either the Manager or CEO

Qualifications / Requirements
> Must be able to work in a professional manner
> Skills with leadership
> Time Management
> STRONG Communication

Bonus Requirements (Optional)
> Experience with Management
